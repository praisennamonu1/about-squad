About us

One thing we all have in common is the Desire to learn and grow.
We will help each other learn by organizing 2-3 squad meetings per week where we brainstorm, research, and solve problems as a squad.
We will uphold Kibo's values by seeking help when we face difficulties that we are unable to tackle, consolidate and share amongst ourselves resources relevant to our field of study, and work on projects together to build team spirit and intelligence.
